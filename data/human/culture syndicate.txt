# "Missions" which add culture events to the spaceport.

#############################################
#      Syndicate with Republic Pairing      #
#############################################

mission "ZZCU Sad Gamblers -S"
	minor
	source
		government "Syndicate"
		not attributes "station"
	to offer
		random < 1
	on offer
		conversation
			`Passing through the spaceport you see a sizable group of people waiting on a lottery draw. You hear a whoop of success and your attention is drawn as one of them wins a moderate sum in a 12th division win; a day or two worth of standard pay.`
			`	It's difficult not to pity them as they immediately buy more tickets with the whole value.`
				decline

mission "ZZCU Abandoned for Disabilities"
	minor
	source
		government "Syndicate"
		not attributes "station"
	to offer
		random < 1
	on offer
		conversation
			`You take a shortcut down an alley and are shocked to find several people with wheelchairs or crutches lving in a shanty town along a hidden service road. It seems Syndicate worlds don't have much in the way of welfare systems. (TBD)`
				decline

	minor
	source
		government "Syndicate"
		not attributes "station"
	to offer
		random < 1

		conversation
			`A couple at a ticket counter kick up a fuss. Turns out there's a tarrif on the luggage, payable by the captain, they need to pay it at this end and it's in excess of the cost of their ticket. (TBD)`
